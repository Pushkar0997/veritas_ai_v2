Our Mission

Build Trust Infrastructure For The Information Age.

Veritas AI is designed as a practical decision layer for readers, journalists, analysts, and platform teams who need fast trust signals without sacrificing context depth.

Our approach combines Longformer-based textual reasoning, retrieval-aware validation patterns, and explainable scoring so users can understand why a claim is flagged.

Core Principle 3

Core Principle 4

Roadmap Highlights

  • Multimodal verification for image-text mismatch detection.
  • Source credibility graphs and publisher reliability scoring.
  • Region and language adaptation for localized fact patterns.
  • Analyst dashboards for newsroom workflows.

Technology Stack

PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers, FastAPI services, Gemini Vision augmentation, and Flask web experience for interactive verification.
